What is this?

Have you ever thought to yourself, "I wish the Bay Area could build more housing units"?
US TOO. As it turns out, every week there are opportunities for you, dear member of the public, to weigh in on whether specific projects should be built. Join our googlegroup to be alerted of opportunities to tell decision makers all over the bay you think it's time to get going with housing production.
"Uhh, can't I just vote on something?" Sure, put your contact info and street address in the form below. If we get enough 'Yes. Build.' voters, we can put something on the ballot. Spread the good word.
