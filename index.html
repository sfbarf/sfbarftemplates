Mission

SFBARF are people who believe SF's housing crisis can be mitigated by increasing the housing supply. We organize renters to testify in favor of new building projects at neighborhood meetings and hearings.

SUPPORT THE E. 12TH & 2ND PROJECT

May 5, 5:30PM

City Council Chamber, 3rd Floor, Oakland City Hall, Oakland City Hall

Oakland is proposing to sell publicly owned land to UrbanCore Development, LLC which they will use to create 300 new housing units. Median rent will be $3000, well below San Francisco average.

The project has many benefits, including 209 dedicated parking spaces, along with parking for over 100 bikes on site in an enclosed secure garage. Additionally, it will REDUCE gentrification by absorbing incoming tenants

It's item 13, on this here agenda, so time your arrival accordingly.
